# 👟 Adidas vs Nike | Europe Market Intelligence Dashboard

> A Business Intelligence Case Study analyzing how Adidas gained momentum over Nike across Europe's key markets using Power BI.

# 📖 Project Overview

This project is a Business Intelligence case study designed to answer one key business question:

> **How did Adidas overtake Nike in Europe, and is this momentum sustainable?**

Rather than creating a traditional sales dashboard, this project applies an analytical approach to understand market trends, competitive positioning, category performance, regional dominance, and channel strategy.

The dashboard analyzes **30 months of European market data** across:

- 🇩🇪 Germany
- 🇬🇧 United Kingdom
- 🇫🇷 France

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

This dashboard answers four strategic business questions:

### 1️⃣ Is Adidas' recent growth structural or just a short-term spike?

Analyze long-term revenue trends to determine whether Adidas' performance is sustainable.

---

### 2️⃣ Does Germany's home-market advantage reflect in sales performance?

Compare country-level revenue to identify regional strengths.

---

### 3️⃣ Is Adidas' growth diversified across categories?

Evaluate whether growth is driven by multiple product categories or concentrated in a few.

---

### 4️⃣ Which brand is better positioned for the Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) market?

Analyze channel performance and identify future business opportunities.

# 📈 Key Insights

✅ Adidas demonstrated sustained revenue growth across the 30-month period.

✅ Germany remains Adidas' strongest market, reinforcing its home-market advantage.

✅ Growth is distributed across multiple product categories rather than relying on a single segment.

✅ Adidas continues strengthening its Direct-to-Consumer presence.

✅ France presents the highest opportunity for future expansion.
